Cold Email Best Practices

Warm up new domains for 2-3 weeks before sending cold emails
Never exceed 4 emails per sequence to the same prospect
Use separate domains from your main business domain to protect deliverability
Multi-channel outreach (email + LinkedIn) can increase booking rates to ~50%
