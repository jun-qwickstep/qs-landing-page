08

Voice
& Tone

We speak like a confident expert who has already done the work. Direct, clear, and always rooted in results.

Direct

Say what you mean. Lead with the outcome. No filler, no corporate fluff.

Expert

Speak with authority. Show deep knowledge without condescension.

Human

Despite working in AI, we talk to humans. Be warm and relatable.

Writing Examples
Write like this
"Your AI voice agent will contact new leads within 60 seconds of opt-in — automatically, every time, 24/7."
Not like this
"Our cutting-edge, revolutionary AI solutions leverage best-in-class technology to synergize your lead generation funnel."
Write like this
"We built a voice agent for B.I.G. Charity Law Group that booked 103 appointments in one month. No human reps needed."
Not like this
"We are passionate about providing transformative, game-changing AI experiences that truly make a difference for all stakeholders."
Writing Rules
Lead with
Results, not features
Sentences
Short. One idea each.
Voice
Active always
Numbers
Use them. They earn trust.
Em dashes
Never. Use periods instead.
Never Use
Buzzwords
"Synergy", "disruptive", "holistic"
Vague claims
Always back with data
Overexplaining
Trust the reader
Overused phrases
"Revolutionary", "cutting-edge"
Exclamation
Only in celebration contexts
